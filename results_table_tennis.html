Man, I love some table tennis, so I was excited to check SPIN out! We arrived mid-day on a Saturday and got stuck on a wait list for a table (which was not too shocking). No matter - we headed to the bar and ordered a drink and some food. The hostess told us that our table would be ready at ~5:45 PM (and told us this estimate should be pretty darn accurate!), so imagine our surprise when I got a text informing us that our table was ready at ~5:05 PM. Our food had literally just arrived and we had to go play some table tennis!

Where do you go and get an endless amount of balls? Here of course! Actually a line from Season 2's 'Nurse Jackie' ("...Ping-Pong at a club Susan Sarandon opened")- brought us here. I Googled that sucker right away and in the middle of watching the episode, hubby searched SPIN, read the info, reserved and paid on the spot. Rest is history.

I'm no Forrest Gump- but I can certainly hold my own with good eye-hand coordination. Hubby always had a knack though for Handball, Paddleball and Racquetball- so Ping Pong somehow came easy for him. If you're on 23rd, you'd never think Ping Pong existed here. Street level entrance gives way to a small waiting area and stairs leading to where it all starts. Restrooms at the bottom of the stairs- plus SPIN's Front Desk to briefly check-in. Beyond that point, place is sprawling and really digging the Underground style that's decked out real nice with an elevated industrial chic. Seating booths and high stools available, plus a colorful Bar towards the back end.
